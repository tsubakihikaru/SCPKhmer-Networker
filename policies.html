Staff Policies & Licensing
CORE DIRECTIVE: AGE POLICY

To be in line with our Age Policy on the main site, you must be at least 14 years old to contribute. This aligns with the Cambodian Age of Responsibility.

Administrative Protocols

  • The 48-Hour Grace Period: Naivety is understandable. If a user is underinformed, they have 48 hours to move their work to external docs before a formal removal.
  • Licensing: All content is compatible with CC BY-SA 3.0. Original artwork is a copyrightable exception, but must be cited correctly.
  • Anti-Whitewashing: Whitewashing media is a secondary illegality. Minimum 5-month ban; maximum permanent ban.
